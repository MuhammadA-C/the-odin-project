Pizza

Description

Recipe for making a cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Shredded cheese
  • Jar of spaghetti sauce
  • Sugar
  • Pizza bread

Steps

  1. Add a bit of sugar to spaghetti sauce and stur it into the sauce
  2. Add spaghetti sauce over pizza bread
  3. Layer shredded cheese ontop of the bread
  4. Place pizza in oven to cook
